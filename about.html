What are we?

Marauders is an exclusive group of investors that provides market analysis, multichain DeFI and NFT alpha, and top-tier networking opportunities among participants. The goal is to foster innovation, intelligent thinking, and global improvement backed by multichain service store.

Profits & Shares

Marauders functions as a tech service provider, offering a wide range of products from social media boosts to custom on-chain development. Services like discord and telegram tools, scraping, and more are available.

Our ecommerce platform showcases and sells these products. Marauders benefit from profit sharing through:

  1. Affiliate Program: Marauders receive personalized coupon codes and affiliations, earning 10% of revenue for each referred customer.
  2. Profit Share: Marauders enjoy 50% of the distributed profit share among all DAO members.
